In a small town by the sea, Lily found an old, weathered bottle washed up on the shore. Inside was a note that read, “Meet me at midnight, where the stars kiss the ocean.”

Curious and restless, she returned that night, heart racing. By the water’s edge, she saw a figure waiting, just out of reach of the waves. As she stepped closer, the figure vanished—leaving only a single shell behind, glowing softly in the moonlight.

Lily picked it up, and for the rest of her life, it whispered the ocean’s secrets to her
