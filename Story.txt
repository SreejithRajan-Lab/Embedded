In a small town by the sea, John found an old, weathered bottle washed up on the shore. Inside... there was a note that read, “Meet me at midnight, where the stars kiss the ocean.”

Curious and restless, he returned that night, heart racing. By the water’s edge, he saw a figure waiting, just out of reach of the waves. As he stepped closer, the figure vanished—leaving only a single shell behind, glowing softly in the moonlight.

John picked it up, and for the rest of her life, it whispered the ocean’s secrets to her
